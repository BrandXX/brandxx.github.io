About TechSoft Systems

Enterprise-grade solutions, neighborhood-style service.

Our Story

TechSoft Systems began in 2012 as a two-person consultancy helping Tucson small businesses untangle networking headaches. Today, we design and support full-stack infrastructures for tribal governments, multifamily operators, and mid-market enterprises across the Southwest. Our founder Johnathan W. Carroll brings more than 20 years of hands-on experience building and rescuing mission-critical environments—from Best Western hotel MSP toolsets to hyper-converged blade clusters that keep tribal services online 24 × 7.

Mission & Vision

  • Mission: Deliver rock-solid, secure, and future-ready IT & AI platforms that let clients innovate without worrying about uptime.
  • Vision: Be the Southwest's most trusted partner for private AI and zero-downtime infrastructure—where every solution is auditable, scalable, and sustainable.
